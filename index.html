About me

Hello! I am Konstantinos Konstantinidis, but I prefer to go by Kostas. I am currently working towards my doctorate degree at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Iowa State University, advised by Professor Aditya Ramamoorthy. My current research involves robust machine learning, speeding up time-intensive distributed computations and alleviating the effects of slow computing devices on distributed setups. I have also worked as a Software Engineering Intern at C3.ai developing the data and ML pipelines for cluster failure prediction.
