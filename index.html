About me

Hello! I am Konstantinos Konstantinidis, but I prefer to go by Kostas. I am working towards my doctorate degree at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Iowa State University, advised by Professor Aditya Ramamoorthy. My current research involves robust machine learning, speeding up time-intensive distributed computations, and alleviating the effects of slow computing devices on distributed setups. Currently, I am a Software Engineer at C3 AI, and specifically, I have been working on the Platform - Data team since September 2022. During Summer 2022, I interned as a Software Engineer at Meta (Facebook) on the App Commerce ML Feature Foundation team implementing a debugging framework for machine learning feature authoring. I have also worked as a Software Engineer Intern at C3 AI, developing the data and ML pipelines for cluster failure prediction. In my free time, I have volunteered as a tutor helping 3rd-grade students in Iowa with math.
